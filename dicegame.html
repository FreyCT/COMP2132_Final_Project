Dice Game

Game Description:
There are 3 rounds in total. Each round will collect 2 dice rolls for the player and computer
Roll Dice: Click to roll your dice. Computer will roll dice immediately after
Point system: The two dice rolls in each round will be summed for total points. Same dice equals to double points
Beware of 1! If 1 is ever rolled for either the player or computer, the round total will autmoatically equate to 0!
